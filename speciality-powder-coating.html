LOW BAKE/FAST CUURE POWDER COATING

As carbon emission is increasing day by day it’s necessary to develop more greener products. Powder Coating is one of the greener products in the coating industry but one of the drawbacks is it requires high temperature for curing. Surfatech Coatings gives a new & innovative solution of Low bake/Fast Cure Powder Coating.

For Low bake Products it requires 165-170 °C temperature for 10 minutes. For Fast cure Products curing time is 5 minutes for 200 °C.

Thin Film Powder Coating

Generally Powder Coating requires minimum 55-60µ of thickness. So compare to conventional liquid coating overall consumption is also high. As powder coating material is costly compare to conventional coating it increases the overall cost. Thus Surfatech Coatings Provides Thin Film Coating which gives excellent finish even at 35-45 µ. This coating is designed at particular particle size distribution to give excellent finish even at low DFT (Dry Film Thickness).

Advantages

  • Low Powder Consumption
  • Reduces overall cost of operation
  • Excellent Finish
  • Excellent Mechanical Properties

Available in

  • Hybrid (Epoxy-Polyester)
  • Pure Polyester

Available in all standard RAL colors. Minimum qty required is 500 Kg.
